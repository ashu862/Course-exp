This Global Candidate Privacy Notice (this "Notice") is a supplement to the UST’s Privacy Notice. Resumes submitted to UST for review shall only include information relevant to job qualifications and employment history including prior employment, relevant positions and dates, significant accomplishments, education, degrees, or certifications obtained, dates and issuing institutions and other relevant awards or achievements or any other information which intentionally shared with, or which is part of your resume.
